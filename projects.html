Things I've built.

A handful of automation workflows and tools, mostly built with n8n and the Claude API. Each one started as a job I wanted doing.

AI News Briefing Pipeline

Pulls AI news from 16 Reddit communities, 6 lab blogs, and a couple of research paper feeds. Claude scores and filters against a personal interest profile. The result lands as a formatted email briefing every morning.

n8n Claude API REST APIs Docker Raspberry Pi SMTP

Job Market Intelligence Pipeline

A daily search across UK job boards for AI automation roles. Claude scores each listing against a detailed professional profile and groups them by realistic fit. The output is a colour-coded digest with application advice and a skills gap radar.
