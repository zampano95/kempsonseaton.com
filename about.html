The short version

It started in Barcelona, with a Python script to automate end-of-shift reporting at a call centre. At Virgin Media O2, it was reverse-engineering Unica's alert system to give thirteen people a clipboard in an environment that didn't have one. At Volkswagen Group, it was an Excel dashboard with live SQL connections to replace a spreadsheet that nobody was filling in.

Currently I'm a Senior CRM Analyst at Nationwide, designing campaigns that reach millions of customers. The same habit shows up here. Internal tools, documentation processes I've rebuilt, and at home, a stack of n8n and Claude pipelines running on a Raspberry Pi.
